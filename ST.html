Stranger Things

Release Date: July 15, 2016

Creators: The Duffer Brothers

Genre: Drama, Fantasy, Horror

Number of Seasons: 4

Rating: 4.8 stars

Set in the 1980s, Stranger Things follows a group of kids in the town of Hawkins as they encounter supernatural phenomena, government conspiracies, and a parallel dimension known as the Upside Down.
