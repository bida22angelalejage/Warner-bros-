Pokémon Go

Developer: Niantic

Publisher: Niantic

Platforms: iOS, Android

Release Date: July 6, 2016

Genre: Augmented Reality, Mobile

Pokémon Go is an augmented reality mobile game that allows players to capture, train, and battle Pokémon creatures in the real world. It became a global phenomenon upon its release and continues to be popular among players of all ages.

Watch Trailer

Key Features

  • Real-world exploration using GPS
  • Capture Pokémon in different locations
  • Battle other players in Gyms
  • Join teams and compete in Raid Battles
  • Trade Pokémon with other players
